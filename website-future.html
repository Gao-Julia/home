Will blue whales grow any larger?

As we now know, blue whales have gradually grown into the largest animals on earth. So what's in store for them in the future?

Sadly, scientists are speculating that whales cannot grow any larger. Whales have been gradually growing larger over time, but they may have reached their physical limit. This is because the larger the whale is, the more time it takes for the whale to close its mouth. Currently, it takes up to 10 seconds for blue whales to close their mouths, but if it takes any longer, their prey will be able to escape.
